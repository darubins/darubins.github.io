Description of Research

I work in tensor-triangular geometry. It is a beautiful, interdisciplinary field with links to algebraic geometry, (stable, equivarient, motivic) homotopy theory, and representation theory. I am currently working to understand the structure of certain "large" motivic derived categories- building off the work of Beren Sanders and his collaborators, and recent computations done by Martin Gallauer
