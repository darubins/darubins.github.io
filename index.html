My research is in the field of Tensor Triangular Geometry. I am currently researching questions related to the structure of various derived categories of motives over algebraically closed fields.

Before coming to UC Santa Cruz I received my B.S. in Mathematics from the University of California, Santa Barbara, as well as a minor in Physics.
